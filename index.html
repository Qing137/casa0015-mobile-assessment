Why Runify?

📱

Too many apps

Users often need separate apps for weather, air quality, maps, and tracking.

❓

Hard to decide

It is not always easy to know whether current conditions are suitable for a run.

📝

No simple record

Keeping a clear running log can be difficult without a dedicated tool.
